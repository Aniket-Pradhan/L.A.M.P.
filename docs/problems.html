Obstacles we faced.

  • The servo was unable to lift greater weight, hence we had to come up with a light weight head.

  • We were unable to find anything known as "Motor Belt", in markets, hence we had to come up with a new idea. We used "Roller Sheets" which are usually discarded in construction sites.

  • A proper and a strong adhesive was required to hold our prototype together. The Hot Glue Gun, was our friend here.

  • A proper covering material was required to cover the "motor belt" and some wires. We came up with simple cloth. However, some other material, which can be streched was required, which we were unable to procure

  • Batteries connected to the H-Bridge discharged out quickly, hence we had to take out some wires out of the lamp to power the H-Bridge from new batteries.

  • Two out of five LED's were not working. Hence, we had to resolder the LED's on the PCB.

What we learnt


  • TEAMWORK, and division of labour

  • Maximum torque beared by a Servo 555
