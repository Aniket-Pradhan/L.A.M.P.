About

The L.A.M.P. is a smart lamp, one of it's kind. It is eco-friendly, as it was made from used parts and waste products. Hence, it is also cheap. It has many also has many features, listed below.

--> Smart gesture switch, to turn it on and off.

--> Light sensed brightness of the lamp.

--> Android App Integration to control the position and angle of the lamp.

--> AND, when you're in the mood, you can activatet the DISCO MODE.
